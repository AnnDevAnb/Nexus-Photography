About Us

At Nexus Wedding Photography, we believe every wedding is a story waiting to be told filled with laughter, emotions, and timeless memories. Based in Trivandrum, we specialize in capturing the magic of love in its purest form, creating photographs you will cherish for a lifetime. Led by Miss Ann Dev Anb and managed by K. B. Deva Anbu (Cinematographer), our team brings together creativity, passion, and professionalism to deliver images that speak beyond words. From intimate candid moments to grand celebrations, we focus on every detail that makes your wedding truly unique. For us, photography isn’t just about pictures it’s about preserving the essence of your journey, your bond, and the unforgettable moments that bring everyone together.
